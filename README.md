# Project6_Fetch

## Necessary Extension
This website has a prerequisite browser extension:

[Allow CORS: Access-Control-Allow-Origin](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/allow-cors-access-control/lhobafahddgcelffkeicbaginigeejlf?hl=en&pli=1)

Chrome is the preferred browser for this exentsion, and further documentation describing how to activate the extension to allow CORS is found in the Chrome Web Store. It is also recommended that this extension only be active during the use of our website.

## Finding Steam IDs

### From the Desktop App
#### To retrieve your own Steam ID:
1. Open up the app
2. Click on your profile dropdown in the top right corner
3. Select 'Account details'
4. Your Steam ID can be found directly under the heading that says "[your username]'s Account"

#### To retrieve a friend's Steam ID:
1. Open up their profile
2. Their ID is the sequence of digits following `https://steamcommunity./com/profiles/`.
You may either type in the ID as you see it, or paste the link into the input box and manually remove all but the digits

### From the Website
#### To retrieve your own Steam ID:
1. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner to access your profile
2. Your ID is the sequence of digits in the brower's url following `steamcommunity./com/profiles/`.

#### To retrieve a friend's Steam ID:
1. Click on your friend's profile to access their account dashboard
2. Their ID is the sequence of digits in the brower's url following `steamcommunity./com/profiles/`.

Note: Game data cannot be accessed if their account settings are set to 'Private'

## Checking Account Privacy Settings
On either the desktop app or the website, follow: `Profile>Edit Profile>Privacy Settings`

In Privacy Settings you must have:
- My profile: Public
- Game details: Public
